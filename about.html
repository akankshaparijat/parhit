About us

A small help by an INDIAN for an INDIAN

Parhit is a platform where we intend to help the people who belongs to underprivileged sections of society especially in India. The vision behind launching Parhit is to make it easier, equitable and accessible for everyone to come and help those who are in need. With the help of this website, it would be easier for different NGOs and rehabilitation centers to access the details and location of beggars, child laborers and other underprivileged people across India.
Anyone with a smartphone in hand can help the one in need while using this website. All you need to do is to visit the website and fill in a form which intends to take the users' information, their location and/or a photograph of the one they want to help. After that you just need to submit the form and automatically the information about the one in need will be accessed by all the nearby NGOs and rehabilitation centers.
