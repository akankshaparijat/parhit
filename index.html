Who Needs Our Help?

This includes the sections of society that needs our consideration
BEGGARS
Begging has grown at a significant rate in India. As per Census 2011, there are 4,13,670 beggars and vagrants in the country. As of now, it is estimated that half a million people in India are beggars.
CHILD LABORS
As per Census 2011, out of 259.6 million child population, 10.1 million are working, either as 'main worker' or as 'marginal worker'. In addition, more than 42.7 million children in India are out of school.
OTHER UNDERPRIVILEGED SECTIONS
Two-thirds of Indias population lives in poverty: 68.8% of the Indian population lives on less than $2 per day. As a result, the Indian subcontinent is one of the poorest countries in the world.
